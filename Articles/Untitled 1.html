Music Picks – Mid September 2023

By: Oven



I have decided to start writing these out on my site so I can get more practice with article stuff in html these are going to be written in libreoffice writer and than exported to my site as html this series of articles will just be to document music I have enjoyed since the last article.



山本東条 (Tojo Yamamoto) – The Mongolian Stomper

Tojo Yamamoto is a post-punk band from Lexington, Kentucky comprised of a number of well established musicians as of writing their self titled ep is still in preorder I have been listening to the only so far out track a lot recently which is called the mongolian stomper I love the tone of the guitar and bass. I am looking forward for its full release

Defiance, Ohio - Midwestern Minutes

Defiance, Ohio is a band that I have loved for a long time but I mainly listened to The Great Depression and mostly wrote off Midwestern Minutes as not all that good other than Hairpool because I enjoyed the pink couch session they did playing that song after recently getting both on vinyl I did a focused listen through of Midwestern Minutes and it grew on me quite a lot I especially have a new found apreciation for You Are Loved, Her Majesty’s Midwestern Islands and Dissimilarity Index.

Busty and the Bass - Eddie



I saw this band live and they did a phenominal preformance not were my music taste typically falls but I really liked them live their album is a bit lower energy than they were live but I think its really great and I copped their album on vinyl at that concert because of how impressive their preformance was





Alternative - What Revolution?

This Ep is just really good old Anarcho-Punk not much to say about it really



AIKO EL GRUPO - Me están apuntando con un arma



This album is in prerelease right now and is entirely spanish langauge I really enjoy it though and might make an effort to figure out the lyrics once its fully out
